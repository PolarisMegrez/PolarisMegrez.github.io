[Phys] 量子场论笔记(3) 克莱因-戈登(Klein-Gordon)场

发表于 2023-10-20 更新于 2024-02-28 分类于 物理 ， 课程笔记

不确定性关系要求我们使用高能物理作为物理学深入微观世界的精确探针.

Klein-Gordon方程

实标量场

\begin{equation} \mathcal{L}(\phi)=\frac{1}{2}(\partial_{\mu}\phi\partial^{\mu}\phi-m^{2}\phi^{2}) \end{equation}

正则动量：
\begin{equation}
\pi(x)=\frac{\partial\mathcal{L}}{\partial\phi}=\dot{\phi}(x)
\end{equation}

\begin{equation} \mathcal{H}(\phi)=\frac{1}{2}(\partial_{\mu}\phi\partial^{\mu}\phi+m^{2}\phi) \end{equation}

平面波解：

\begin{equation} \phi(t,\,\vec{x})=\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}p\,\bar{N}_{p}(a_{p}e^{+\mathrm{i}(\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x}-\omega_{p}t)}+a_{p}^{\dagger}e^{-\mathrm{i}(\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x}-\omega_{p}t)})} \end{equation}

此处 Nˉp\bar{N}_{p}Nˉp​ 为归一化常数， ωp=p⃗2+m2\omega_{p}=\sqrt{\vec{p}^{2}+m^{2}}ωp​=p​2+m2​ 。

计算可得

\begin{equation} \pi(t,\,\vec{x})=\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}p\,\bar{N}_{p}(-\mathrm{i}\omega_{p}a_{p}e^{+\mathrm{i}(\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x}-\omega_{p}t)}+\mathrm{i}\omega_{p}a_{p}^{\dagger}e^{-\mathrm{i}(\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x}-\omega_{p}t)})} \end{equation}

则有

\begin{align} \omega_{p}\phi+\mathrm{i}\pi=\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}p\,(2\bar{N}_{p}\omega_{p})a_{p}e^{+\mathrm{i}(\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x}-\omega_{p}t)}}\\ \omega_{p}\phi-\mathrm{i}\pi=\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}p\,(2\bar{N}_{p}\omega_{p})a_{p}^{\dagger}e^{-\mathrm{i}(\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x}-\omega_{p}t)}} \end{align}

作Fourier逆变换得到

\begin{align} (2\bar{N}_{p}\omega_{p})a_{p}e^{-\mathrm{i}\omega_{p}t}=\frac{\bar{N}_{p}}{2\omega_{}(2\pi)^{3}}\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}x\,e^{-\mathrm{i}\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x}}(\omega_{p}\phi+\mathrm{i}\pi)}\\ (2\bar{N}_{p}\omega_{p})a_{p}^{\dagger}e^{+\mathrm{i}\omega_{p}t}=\frac{\bar{N}_{p}}{2\omega_{}(2\pi)^{3}}\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}x\,e^{+\mathrm{i}\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x}}(\omega_{p}\phi-\mathrm{i}\pi)} \end{align}

从而有

\begin{align} a_{p}=\bar{A}_{p}\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}x\,e^{+\mathrm{i}(\omega_{p}t-\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x})}(\omega_{p}\phi+\mathrm{i}\pi)}\\ a_{p}^{\dagger}=\bar{A}_{p}\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}x\,e^{-\mathrm{i}(\omega_{p}t-\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x})}(\omega_{p}\phi-\mathrm{i}\pi)} \end{align}

此处 Aˉp\bar{A}_{p}Aˉp​ 为归一化常数，满足关系

\begin{equation} \bar{A}_{p}\bar{N}_{p}=\frac{1}{2\omega_{p}(2\pi)^{3}} \end{equation}

注意到正则对易关系

\begin{equation} [\phi(t,\,\vec{x}),\,\pi(t,\,\vec{x}')]=\mathrm{i}\delta^{3}(\vec{x}-\vec{x}') \end{equation}

此处

\begin{equation} \delta(\vec{x}-\vec{x}')=\frac{1}{(2\pi)^{3}}\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}p\,e^{\pm\mathrm{i}\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x}}} \end{equation}

解得产生、湮灭算符的对易关系

\begin{align} [a_{p},\,a_{p'}^{\dagger}]&=\bar{A}_{p}\bar{A}_{p'}\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}x\,\mathrm{d}^{3}x'\,e^{+\mathrm{i}(\omega_{p}t-\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x})}[\omega_{p}\phi+\mathrm{i}\pi,\,\omega_{p'}\phi-\mathrm{i}\pi]e^{-\mathrm{i}(\omega_{p'}t-\vec{p'}\cdot\vec{x'})}}\\ &=(\omega_{p'}+\omega_{p})\bar{A}_{p}\bar{A}_{p'}\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}x\,\mathrm{d}^{3}x'\,e^{+\mathrm{i}(\omega_{p}t-\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x})}[\phi,\,\pi]e^{-\mathrm{i}(\omega_{p'}t-\vec{p'}\cdot\vec{x'})}}\\ &=(\omega_{p'}+\omega_{p})\bar{A}_{p}\bar{A}_{p'}e^{+\mathrm{i}(\omega_{p}-\omega_{p'})t}\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}x\,e^{-\mathrm{i}(\vec{p}-\vec{p}')\cdot\vec{x}}}\\ &=(2\pi)^{3}(\omega_{p'}+\omega_{p})\bar{A}_{p}\bar{A}_{p'}\delta^{3}(\vec{p}-\vec{p}') \end{align}

注意到，倘若我们选取归一化常数

\begin{equation} \bar{N}_{p}=\bar{A}_{p}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2\omega_{p}(2\pi)^{3}}} \end{equation}

则有

\begin{equation} \left.\begin{array}{r} \displaystyle a_{p}=\bar{N}_{p}\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}x\,e^{+\mathrm{i}(\omega_{p}t-\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x})}(\omega_{p}\phi+\mathrm{i}\pi)}\\ \displaystyle a_{p}^{\dagger}=\bar{N}_{p}\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}x\,e^{-\mathrm{i}(\omega_{p}t-\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x})}(\omega_{p}\phi-\mathrm{i}\pi)} \end{array}\right\}\quad\Rightarrow\quad[a_{p},\,a_{p'}^{\dagger}]=\delta^{3}(\vec{p}-\vec{p}') \end{equation}

事实上，同样可以选取其他归一化方法，使得Nˉp\bar{N}_{p}Nˉp​的取值更加适合场量平均值的计算，我们之后一律采取(19)式所示的归一化方法。
