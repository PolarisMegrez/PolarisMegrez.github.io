开放量子系统与Lindblad主方程

Schrödinger方程描述封闭量子系统的动力学演化，其概率流密度j(r⃗, t)\boldsymbol{j}(\vec{r},\,t)j(r,t)满足守恒方程

\begin{equation} \frac{\partial}{\partial t}|\Psi(\vec{r},\,t)|^{2}+\nabla\cdot\boldsymbol{j}=0 \end{equation}

代表封闭系统的粒子数守恒，不存在泵浦或损耗。

然而实际研究中可能需要处理开放量子系统，亦即系统与环境存在相互作用，研究开放系统的方法有Lindblad主方程(master equation)、福克-普朗克方程(Fokker-Planck)、量子朗之万方程(Herisenberg-Langevin)等，我们介绍Lindblad主方程。

von Neumann方程

假设系统SSS(ystem)处于热平衡环境中，环境EEE(nvironment)可以视为一个粒子数远大于系统的热库RRR(eservoir)或热浴BBB(ath)，从而系统与环境的相互作用对环境的影响近似为零。

不妨记系统HS\mathcal{H}_{S}HS​的密度算符为ρ^(t)\hat{\rho}(t)ρ^​(t)，体系HS⊗HR\mathcal{H}_{S}\otimes\mathcal{H}_{R}HS​⊗HR​的密度算符为χ^(t)\hat{\chi}(t)χ^​(t)，我们关心系统的演化，可以通过χ^(t)\hat{\chi}(t)χ^​(t)对环境HR\mathcal{H}_{R}HR​求迹得到

\begin{equation} \hat{\rho}(t)=\mathrm{Tr}_{R}\,\hat{\chi}(t) \end{equation}

系统和环境组成的体系(S⊕R)(S\oplus R)(S⊕R)可以视为孤立系统，其Hamiltonian记为

\begin{equation} \hat{H}=\hat{H}_{S}+\hat{H}_{R}+\hat{H}_{SR} \end{equation}

此处HS, HRH_{S},\,H_{R}HS​,HR​分别代表系统与环境的自由演化，H^SR\hat{H}_{SR}H^SR​代表二者的相互作用。从而有von Neumann方程

\begin{equation} \frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}t}\hat{\chi}(t)=\frac{1}{\mathrm{i}\hbar}[\hat{H},\,\hat{\chi}(t)] \end{equation}

转换到Dirac绘景（相互作用绘景），选取H^0=H^S+H^R\hat{H}_{0}=\hat{H}_{S}+\hat{H}_{R}H^0​=H^S​+H^R​，则有

\begin{align} \hat{\chi}^{(I)}(t)&=e^{(\mathrm{i}/\hbar)\hat{H}_{0}t}\hat{\chi}(t)e^{-(\mathrm{i}/\hbar)\hat{H}_{0}t}\\ \hat{H}_{SR}^{(I)}(t)&=e^{(\mathrm{i}/\hbar)\hat{H}_{0}t}\hat{H}_{SR}(t)e^{-(\mathrm{i}/\hbar)\hat{H}_{0}t} \end{align}

上述von Neumann方程转化为

\begin{equation} \frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}t}\hat{\chi}^{(I)}(t)=\frac{1}{\mathrm{i}\hbar}[\hat{H}_{SR}^{(I)}(t),\,\hat{\chi}^{(I)}(t)] \end{equation}

系统与环境的相互作用算符

求解演化方程(7)必须得到H^SR\hat{H}_{SR}H^SR​的具体形式，我们假设环境是电磁场或者晶体中的声子场，从而其Hamiltonian可以表示为一系列谐振子的线性组合

\begin{equation} \hat{H}_{R}=\sum_{k}{\Omega_{k}\hat{\Gamma}_{k}^{\dagger}\hat{\Gamma}_{k}} \end{equation}

此处频率Ωk\Omega_{k}Ωk​随着模式kkk而变化，Γ^k\hat{\Gamma}_{k}Γ^k​是对应的Boson算符。从而相互作用可以表示为系统算符s^i\hat{s}_{i}s^i​与环境算符Γi\Gamma_{i}Γi​的线性组合

\begin{equation} \hat{H}_{SR}=\hbar\sum_{i}{\big(\hat{s}_{i}^{\dagger}\hat{\Gamma}_{i}+\hat{s}_{i}\hat{\Gamma}_{i}^{\dagger}\big)} \end{equation}

变换到Dirac绘景，则有

\begin{align} \hat{H}_{SR}^{(I)}(t)&=\hbar\sum_{i}{e^{(\mathrm{i}/\hbar)(\hat{H}_{S}+\hat{H}_{R})t}\big(\hat{s}_{i}^{\dagger}\hat{\Gamma}_{i}+\hat{s}_{i}\hat{\Gamma}_{i}^{\dagger}\big)e^{-(\mathrm{i}/\hbar)(\hat{H}_{S}+\hat{H}_{R})t}}\\ &=\hbar\sum_{i}{\big(e^{(\mathrm{i}/\hbar)\hat{H}_{S}t}\hat{s}_{i}^{\dagger}e^{-(\mathrm{i}/\hbar)\hat{H}_{S}t}\big)\big(e^{(\mathrm{i}/\hbar)\hat{H}_{R}t}\hat{\Gamma}_{i}e^{-(\mathrm{i}/\hbar)\hat{H}_{R}t}\big)}+h. c.\\ &=\hbar\sum_{i}{\big(\hat{s}_{i}^{\dagger(I)}\hat{\Gamma}_{i}^{(I)}+\hat{s}_{i}^{(I)}\hat{\Gamma}_{i}^{\dagger(I)}\big)} \end{align}

我们假设存在某“初始”时刻系统与环境没有关联，从而体系的密度算符可以分解为

\begin{equation} \hat{\chi}^{(I)}(0)=\hat{\chi}(0)=\hat{\rho}(0)\otimes\hat{R}_{0} \end{equation}

此处R^0\hat{R}_{0}R^0​是环境初始状态的密度算符，一般假设其满足条件

\begin{equation} \mathrm{Tr}_{R}{\hat{H}_{SR}^{(I)}} \end{equation}

Born近似/弱耦合近似

随着体系演化，系统与环境由于相互作用产生关联，我们假设二者的相互作用较弱

此处演化方程(7)无法直接求解，倘若将H^SR\hat{H}_{SR}H^SR​视为微扰，则有迭代解法。方程(7)积分得到

\begin{equation} \hat{\chi}^{(I)}(t)=\hat{\chi}^{(I)}(0)+\frac{1}{\mathrm{i}\hbar}\int_{0}^{t}{[\hat{H}_{SR}^{(I)}(\tau),\,\hat{\chi}^{(I)}(\tau)]\,\mathrm{d}\tau} \end{equation}

Klein-Gordon方程

实标量场

\begin{equation} \mathcal{L}(\phi)=\frac{1}{2}(\partial_{\mu}\phi\partial^{\mu}\phi-m^{2}\phi^{2}) \end{equation}

正则动量：
\begin{equation}
\pi(x)=\frac{\partial\mathcal{L}}{\partial\phi}=\dot{\phi}(x)
\end{equation}

\begin{equation} \mathcal{H}(\phi)=\frac{1}{2}(\partial_{\mu}\phi\partial^{\mu}\phi+m^{2}\phi) \end{equation}

平面波解：

\begin{equation} \phi(t,\,\vec{x})=\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}p\,\bar{N}_{p}(a_{p}e^{+\mathrm{i}(\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x}-\omega_{p}t)}+a_{p}^{\dagger}e^{-\mathrm{i}(\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x}-\omega_{p}t)})} \end{equation}

此处 Nˉp\bar{N}_{p}Nˉp​ 为归一化常数， ωp=p⃗2+m2\omega_{p}=\sqrt{\vec{p}^{2}+m^{2}}ωp​=p​2+m2​ 。

计算可得

\begin{equation} \pi(t,\,\vec{x})=\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}p\,\bar{N}_{p}(-\mathrm{i}\omega_{p}a_{p}e^{+\mathrm{i}(\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x}-\omega_{p}t)}+\mathrm{i}\omega_{p}a_{p}^{\dagger}e^{-\mathrm{i}(\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x}-\omega_{p}t)})} \end{equation}

则有

\begin{align} \omega_{p}\phi+\mathrm{i}\pi=\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}p\,(2\bar{N}_{p}\omega_{p})a_{p}e^{+\mathrm{i}(\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x}-\omega_{p}t)}}\\ \omega_{p}\phi-\mathrm{i}\pi=\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}p\,(2\bar{N}_{p}\omega_{p})a_{p}^{\dagger}e^{-\mathrm{i}(\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x}-\omega_{p}t)}} \end{align}

作Fourier逆变换得到

\begin{align} (2\bar{N}_{p}\omega_{p})a_{p}e^{-\mathrm{i}\omega_{p}t}=\frac{\bar{N}_{p}}{2\omega_{}(2\pi)^{3}}\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}x\,e^{-\mathrm{i}\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x}}(\omega_{p}\phi+\mathrm{i}\pi)}\\ (2\bar{N}_{p}\omega_{p})a_{p}^{\dagger}e^{+\mathrm{i}\omega_{p}t}=\frac{\bar{N}_{p}}{2\omega_{}(2\pi)^{3}}\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}x\,e^{+\mathrm{i}\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x}}(\omega_{p}\phi-\mathrm{i}\pi)} \end{align}

从而有

\begin{align} a_{p}=\bar{A}_{p}\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}x\,e^{+\mathrm{i}(\omega_{p}t-\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x})}(\omega_{p}\phi+\mathrm{i}\pi)}\\ a_{p}^{\dagger}=\bar{A}_{p}\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}x\,e^{-\mathrm{i}(\omega_{p}t-\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x})}(\omega_{p}\phi-\mathrm{i}\pi)} \end{align}

此处 Aˉp\bar{A}_{p}Aˉp​ 为归一化常数，满足关系

\begin{equation} \bar{A}_{p}\bar{N}_{p}=\frac{1}{2\omega_{p}(2\pi)^{3}} \end{equation}

注意到正则对易关系

\begin{equation} [\phi(t,\,\vec{x}),\,\pi(t,\,\vec{x}')]=\mathrm{i}\delta^{3}(\vec{x}-\vec{x}') \end{equation}

此处

\begin{equation} \delta(\vec{x}-\vec{x}')=\frac{1}{(2\pi)^{3}}\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}p\,e^{\pm\mathrm{i}\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x}}} \end{equation}

解得产生、湮灭算符的对易关系

\begin{align} [a_{p},\,a_{p'}^{\dagger}]&=\bar{A}_{p}\bar{A}_{p'}\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}x\,\mathrm{d}^{3}x'\,e^{+\mathrm{i}(\omega_{p}t-\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x})}[\omega_{p}\phi+\mathrm{i}\pi,\,\omega_{p'}\phi-\mathrm{i}\pi]e^{-\mathrm{i}(\omega_{p'}t-\vec{p'}\cdot\vec{x'})}}\\ &=(\omega_{p'}+\omega_{p})\bar{A}_{p}\bar{A}_{p'}\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}x\,\mathrm{d}^{3}x'\,e^{+\mathrm{i}(\omega_{p}t-\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x})}[\phi,\,\pi]e^{-\mathrm{i}(\omega_{p'}t-\vec{p'}\cdot\vec{x'})}}\\ &=(\omega_{p'}+\omega_{p})\bar{A}_{p}\bar{A}_{p'}e^{+\mathrm{i}(\omega_{p}-\omega_{p'})t}\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}x\,e^{-\mathrm{i}(\vec{p}-\vec{p}')\cdot\vec{x}}}\\ &=(2\pi)^{3}(\omega_{p'}+\omega_{p})\bar{A}_{p}\bar{A}_{p'}\delta^{3}(\vec{p}-\vec{p}') \end{align}

注意到，倘若我们选取归一化常数

\begin{equation} \bar{N}_{p}=\bar{A}_{p}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2\omega_{p}(2\pi)^{3}}} \end{equation}

则有

\begin{equation} \left.\begin{array}{r} \displaystyle a_{p}=\bar{N}_{p}\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}x\,e^{+\mathrm{i}(\omega_{p}t-\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x})}(\omega_{p}\phi+\mathrm{i}\pi)}\\ \displaystyle a_{p}^{\dagger}=\bar{N}_{p}\int{\mathrm{d}^{3}x\,e^{-\mathrm{i}(\omega_{p}t-\vec{p}\cdot\vec{x})}(\omega_{p}\phi-\mathrm{i}\pi)} \end{array}\right\}\quad\Rightarrow\quad[a_{p},\,a_{p'}^{\dagger}]=\delta^{3}(\vec{p}-\vec{p}') \end{equation}
